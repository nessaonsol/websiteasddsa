Sophie AI

What is Sophie?

Sophie is an AI that creates videos from text. Just tell Sophie what you want to see, and she'll make it into a video for you.

Powered by Veo 3 - Google's latest video AI technology

What Sophie Can Do

  • Turn any text description into a video
  • Create videos up to 60 seconds long
  • Make videos in HD quality (1920x1080)
  • Generate realistic or artistic styles
  • Process videos in about 1-2 minutes

How It Works

Sophie uses Google's Veo 3 model to understand your text and create matching videos. Here's the simple process:

Step 1: Write Your Prompt

Describe what you want to see in your video. Be specific about what's happening, where it takes place, and how it should look.

Step 2: Sophie Creates

Sophie's AI processes your description and generates a unique video based on your prompt.

Step 3: Download Your Video

In 1-2 minutes, your video is ready to download and use however you'd like.

Example Prompts

Here are some examples of what you can ask Sophie to create:

"A cat playing with a ball of yarn in a sunny living room" "Ocean waves crashing on a beach at sunset" "A person walking through a busy city street at night" "Time-lapse of flowers blooming in a garden"

Video Specifications

Feature Details
Max Length 60 seconds
Resolution 1920x1080 (HD)
Generation Time 1-2 minutes
File Format MP4

Tips for Better Results

  • Be specific: Include details about lighting, colors, and mood
  • Describe movement: Mention if things should be moving slowly or quickly
  • Set the scene: Tell Sophie where the action is happening
  • Keep it simple: One main idea per video works best

Good Prompt Example

"A golden retriever running through a green meadow on a sunny day, with wildflowers swaying in the breeze"

This prompt works well because it describes the subject (golden retriever), action (running), setting (green meadow), weather (sunny), and extra details (wildflowers, breeze).
